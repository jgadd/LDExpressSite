About Us

LD Express Limited is a nationally owned company under Rhicornese Holdings Limited that specializes in the provision of ‘one stop shop’ nationwide freight forwarding, logistical, and courier services with the aim of satisfying the requirements of our clients, both individual and corporate.
The company was registered under the Investment Promotion Authority (IPA) on February 04th, 2016, and has since grown expanding its scope of operations throughout Papua New Guinea (PNG) maintaining branches in four (4) provinces across the country including the National Capital District (Port Moresby), Morobe Province (Lae), Madang Province (Madang), East Sepik Province (Wewak), and East New Britain Province (Kokopo).

Meet Our Management Team

Our management team comprises of very skilled, experienced, and dedicated managers who have between them more than 30 years of hands-on experience both in business management, project logistics, freight forwarding, trucking, customs operating procedures and commercial business activities.

He has established four subsidiaries under Rhicornese Holdings including LD Express.
